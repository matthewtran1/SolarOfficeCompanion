Our Vision

Our team's ultimate goal is to provide an innovate solution to help combat climate change and promote health and safety
The team aims to design a relatively small stand-alone photovoltaic system for consumer use with the following requirements:

Provide clean and renewable energy for personal office use Supply low voltage DC to power consumer electronics Use photovoltaic (PV) cells to provide clean energy

Design

The proposed design is a modular system that consists of a charger controller, solar panel, and battery bank
This system is currently designed for use with DC electronics only and aimed at office workers and home integration
